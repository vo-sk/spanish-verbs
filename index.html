🎤 Record & Score

  • Green Record button: Tap to start recording
  • Speak the Spanish phrase clearly into your microphone
  • Instant feedback: See your score (0-100%)
  • Color-coded words:
    • Green 90%+ = Excellent
    • Blue 70%+ = Good
    • Yellow 50%+ = Fair
    • Red <50% = Needs practice

📊 Track Your Progress

  • Verb Score: See your average % under each verb name
  • Attempts tracked: Number of recordings you've made
  • Saved automatically: Progress persists in your browser
  • Goal: Aim for 90%+ on all verbs!

💡 Tips for Success

  • 🎧 Use headphones for better audio quality

  • 🗣️ Speak clearly and at normal pace
  • 🔁 Practice difficult verbs multiple times
  • ⭐ Try to improve your average score each time
